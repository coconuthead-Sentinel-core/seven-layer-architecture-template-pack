ADR / MADR DECISION RECORD TEMPLATE

Use for
- Recording one meaningful architecture decision per file or per section.
- Explaining why a decision was made and what tradeoffs were accepted.

File naming recommendation
- YYYYMMDD-short-decision-title.txt

Template
# {ADR Title}

## Context And Problem Statement
- {what_problem_are_we_solving}
- {drivers_constraints}

## Decision Drivers
- {driver_1}
- {driver_2}

## Considered Options
1. {option_1}
2. {option_2}
3. {option_3}

## Decision Outcome
Chosen option: {option_n}

### Positive Consequences
- {pro_1}

### Negative Consequences
- {con_1}

## Pros And Cons Of The Options
### {option_1}
- Pros:
  - {pro}
- Cons:
  - {con}

### {option_2}
- Pros:
  - {pro}
- Cons:
  - {con}

### {option_3}
- Pros:
  - {pro}
- Cons:
  - {con}

## Links
- Related ADRs: {links}
- Related docs: {links}

Notes
- Keep each ADR small and specific.
- Create multiple ADRs instead of one giant decision log.
